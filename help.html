As this is your first time using MPN10 take a little time to familiarise yourself with how it works...

1) Select the "SCORE" option

Select 'SCORE' to rate each of your symptoms from 0 to 10.

2) Score each symptom

The system defaults to 0 on your first visit and will calculate your answer as 0 unless you change it. After the first visit it will default to your last saved score setting.

3) Save your symptom score

When you tap 'SAVE & CONTINUE' you will be taken to the next symptom. Once all 10 syptoms have a score entered you will be automatically taken to a session review screen.

4) Review your session

Before your scores are submitted and added to your tracker data you can review and edit each individual score if you wish to.

5) View your scores

Once submitted your session scores are entered into your tracker as the latest vertical column on the right of your chart. On this screen you can see your progress and share what you are looking at with your HCP. The system defaults to the last 8 sessions submitted but you can scroll sideways through all the data you have ever inputted. PLEASE NOTE: your HCP will be sent whichever time period of results you are currently viewing on the screen.
